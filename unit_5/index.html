About Carl Khor

Carl Khor was born and raised in Malaysia. He migrated to the United States when he was 20 and attended University of Texas, Austin. Carl had a successful career as a certified actuary and is now retired from climbing the corporate ladder where he held positions as high as Cheif Financial Officer. He is also a certified badminton coach and has coached his two sons to be Junior National champions. In his free time, he hangs out with his beautiful wife, Allyssa Khor, plays and coaches badminton in the bay area, wood works, or hangs out with his sons. Carl picked up spray painting as a hobby a year ago and loves spreading the art of spray paint to others.
